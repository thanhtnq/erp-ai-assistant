## Company Identity

- **Company:** TNO Systems Pte Ltd — Singapore-based ERP software provider.
- **Product:** Globe3 ERP — cloud-based, web-based, scalable, integrated ERP solution.
- **ERP business since:** 2002.
- **Deployment scale:** Official materials indicate hundreds to over a thousand deployments, with 10,000+ users across 10+ countries.
- **Markets:** At least Singapore and Malaysia, with claimed coverage of 10+ countries.

## Product Overview

Globe3 ERP integrates core business processes including finance, inventory, supply chain, project management, CRM, and HR into one system. It targets SMEs and mid-range organizations, especially those with multi-branch, multi-location operations.

**Target customers:**
- SMEs ranging from 20 to 1,000 employees
- Multi-branch, multi-location companies
- Industries: construction, trading, distribution, manufacturing, services, equipment rental, field service, retail, marine & offshore

## Services

TNO Systems offers four categories of service:

1. **Business Solution Consultancy** — customer-centric approach with business, accounting, finance, and technical expertise since 2002
2. **Customization** — tailored to client requirements
3. **Implementation Services** — phased approach; standard-scope projects typically take around 2 to 3 months
4. **Support & Maintenance** — online support via Skype or TeamViewer, telephone support, remote desktop troubleshooting, continuous quality checks, recorded transactions for transparency

## Ready-to-Use Answer Templates

### "What is TNO Systems?"
TNO Systems Pte Ltd is a Singapore-based ERP software provider. Its flagship product is Globe3 ERP, a cloud-based and web-based integrated ERP solution for industries such as construction, trading, manufacturing, services, and equipment rental.

### "What is Globe3 ERP?"
Globe3 ERP is TNO Systems' integrated business management software that combines modules such as finance, inventory, supply chain, project management, CRM, and HR into one system.

### "Who is Globe3 ERP for?"
Globe3 ERP targets SMEs and multi-branch organizations, especially in trading, distribution, construction, service, and engineering-related industries. The FAQ mentions organizations ranging from 20 to 1,000 employees.

### "Does Globe3 support multi-company or multi-language?"
Yes. Globe3 ERP supports multi-company and multi-language environments for organizations with branches across different locations.

### "Can Globe3 export reports?"
Yes. Reports can be exported in Excel, PDF, and CSV formats.

### "How long does implementation take?"
Implementation is done in phases. If requirements are close to the standard modules, implementation can take around 2 to 3 months.

### "Does Globe3 have grant support in Singapore?"
Yes. Globe3 ERP is listed as a pre-approved PSG solution, and eligible SMEs may receive up to 50% PSG support. For eligibility confirmation, please contact the sales team or check the official PSG channels.

### "How do I contact sales or support?"
Sales enquiries: enquiry@globe3.com, +65 6749 8823, or WhatsApp +65 8060 9925. Customer support: support@globe3.com or +65 6631 8550, Monday to Friday, 9 a.m. to 6 p.m.
